  • Each player chooses a class and a corner to start in
  • Choose a boss and take the boss's stats card
  • Grab the specified number of each tile needed
  • Take turns placing down a tile on the board, each tile must be connected
  • Go until the entire board is filled and there are no tiles left
  • Place each of your characters in your chosen corner

  • Roll a 6-sided die to determine the number of moves.
  • Move that many tiles in any cardinal direction of your choosing.
  • If you land on a tile, trigger the effect:
    • Event - draw event card
    • Encounter - draw enemy card
    • Trap - draw trap card
    • Merchant - draw merchant card
  • If you land on a player, fight:
    • Both players roll for initiative, higher number goes first
    • Do however much damage you roll after applying skills
    • Upon killing the other player, take all their gold
  • Monster fights work similar to player fights:
    • Monsters have no skills to apply
    • Use the stats on the monster card to determine damage and HP
