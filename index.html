Dungeon Crawler

In this competitive dungeon crawler for 2-4 players, choose your class - Warrior, Rogue, Archer, or Mage - each with unique abilities that dramatically impact gameplay. Roll dice to navigate through procedurally generated dungeons filled with events, encounters, merchants, and traps.

Battle monsters, collect treasure, and sabotage fellow adventurers in your quest to reach and defeat the final boss. Will you play charge headlong as the Warrior, sneak and backstab with the Rogue, rain arrows with the Archer, or control fate itself with the Mage?
